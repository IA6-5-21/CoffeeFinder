Information


We are 4 students who have written a bachelor thesis on level measurement with computer vision, machine learning and traditional image processing. We study at the university in southeastern Norway campus Posrgunn. Bouvet has an in-house coffee project and wanted some students that could help solve a sub-thesis from the coffee project, where we were lucky to be able to use this as our bachelor thesis.

The task consists of measuring the level in a coffee tank of a coffee machine, where we will use computer vision, traditional image processing and machine learning. There will be a comparison of both models on this website, where you can see which level gives the most correct answer.

Click here to test yourself

For the machine learning model, we use the FastAI library and the PyTorch library. With traditional image processing, we use the OpenCV library with Python.

About us

We are four students from the University of Southeast Norway who are taking a bachelor's degree in computer science and automation. Everyone enjoys programming very much and has gained some programming experience during these three years at school. None of us had any particular knowledge of machine learning, traditional image processing or computer vision. It has been a fun challenge to achieve this, where the learning curve has been high.
