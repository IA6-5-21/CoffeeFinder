Information


This website is the result from a bachelor project at University of South-Eastern Norway where we were lucky to get a collaboration with Bouvet. Bouvet has an in-house project to develop a fully automated AI-barista with a robot arm, and wanted some students that could help solve a sub-thesis from the coffee project. To be able to do this they needed a method to measure the level of coffee beans in the barista. The task they gave us was to use computer vision and compare level measurement with a trained machine learning model against traditional image processing. Due to the Corona situation in 2020/21 the coffee robot is inaccessible, and a separate test rig must also be made to be able to train and test the models.

The task consists of measuring the level in a coffee tank of a coffee machine, where we will use computer vision with machine learning and traditional image processing. There will be a comparison of both models on this website. For the machine learning model, we use the FastAI library and the PyTorch library. With traditional image processing, we use the OpenCV library with Python.

About us

We are four students from the University of South-Eastern Norway who are taking a bachelor's degree in computer science and automation. Everyone enjoys programming very much and has gained some programming experience during these three years at school. None of us had any particular knowledge of machine learning, traditional image processing or computer vision. It has been a fun challenge to achieve this, where the learning curve has been high.
